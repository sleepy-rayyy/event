Rules & Regulations

All participants are expected to follow these guidelines throughout the event. Fair play, teamwork, and respect are mandatory for all.

General Rules

  • All teams must register before the official deadline.
  • Participants should wear their assigned uniforms or IDs at all times.
  • No use of foul language or unsportsmanlike behavior will be tolerated.
  • Teams must arrive 15 minutes before their scheduled match.

Game Conduct

  • Referees’ decisions are final and must be respected.
  • Cheating, exploiting bugs, or unfair tactics are strictly prohibited.
  • Any form of physical or verbal abuse will result in disqualification.
  • Teams must maintain proper decorum both online and offline.

Mobile Game Rules

  • Players must use only registered accounts and devices.
  • Network disconnections during a match are the player’s responsibility.
  • Any form of boosting or third-party software is not allowed.
  • Each team captain must submit match results immediately after the game.

Penalties

  • Violation of any rule may lead to warnings or disqualification.
  • Repeated offenses will result in team suspension from future events.
  • The event committee reserves the right to modify rules if necessary.

By participating, you agree to follow all rules and decisions made by the event organizers.
